About Us

This program is designed to streamline the process of alliteration research for scholars worldwide. By automating the identification and extraction of alliterative phrases from digital texts, it significantly reduces the time and effort required for manual analysis. This innovative tool empowers researchers to delve deeper into the study of alliteration, uncovering new patterns, trends, and insights within a vast corpus of digital literature

This project is financially supported by Grant-in-Aid for Scientific Research (KAKENHI: Project No. 22K00426).
